Who is Jacob Møller

In the year of 1982 I came into this world.

In the year of 2014 I made this web page.

In the year of 2014 you visited my web page. Thank you for that! You are welcome and I hope you enjoy it here.

2014 is also the year of which I graduated my Bachelor of science in engineering.

In the period 1997-2005 I did almost nothing but play music in various rock bands apart from working sometimes as a sound engineer.

2005 is the year I started dating my girlfriend Lærke.

2006 is where my first born daughter Johanne came in to this world. She was followed by here sister Maja in 2009.

From 2006 I started working as a sound engineer as a day job, doing various concerts and other events. Which now has led me to become an electrical engineer.
